Brain MRI Tumor Classification

CNN-based pipeline to classify tumor presence in MRI slices with a focus on reproducible training and transparent evaluation.

Tools: PyTorch, Python Role: End-to-end ML Status: Active iteration

Problem

Build a reliable classifier that can flag tumor presence in brain MRI slices while handling typical medical imaging constraints.

Approach

  • Cleaned and normalized MRI slices for consistent training.
  • Applied augmentation to improve generalization.
  • Tracked evaluation metrics and error patterns.

Results

Documented baseline performance and qualitative checks. Final metrics can be added once locked.

Next Steps
